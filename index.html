Location Based Alerts

By attaching a small device, called a GPS tracker, on some part of your pet’s body, you are given the chance to get alerts every time your pet enters or leave locations that you have previously set up on the map. Thus, you can control where your pet goes, therefore making sure that it doesn’t get lost or stolen.

GEOTrax gives your pets not only protection, but also freedom.
BreadCrumb Tracking

Our strategic partnership with ADT Security provides you with a 24/7 security team standing by monitoring your every action to ensure you are safe. ADT Security agents are trained on ways to recognize "mistakes" or real-world danger - and then to use their professional training in order to make a determination regarding whether the police should be called. Should yourGEOSafety app turn on live audio or video feeds, it is their job to listen or watch the situation and make a determination as to the danger. This option prevents possibly embarrassing situations that could occur if your monitoring system were to be sent to friends or family members - who are more interested in "snooping" into your life vs. your personal security.

Augmented Reality

Whenever you lose your pet, find it immediately by pointing your phone in some general direction of where you think that your pet might be. Then you will see an icon which indicating whether your pet is there. If you have lost your pet in an urban area, details about the surrounding businesses will also be shown on screen, allowing you to call and ask if anyone has seen your pet.

Missing Pet Pet Poster

Should you lose your pet, you can inform your family, friends, and the world about your missing pet all at once. We are giving you the chance to fill in information about your pet – colour, size, type of animal, breed, and also fill in information about the place and time where the pet was last seen, as well as a photo. You can share this information on the popular social websites, you can send it to your friends or you can have it printed.

Navigator

Your child is crying because his/her best fluffy friend is missing and you don’t know what to do. The situation is tense and stressful, and you have only your smartphone device by your side. Well, use it – that’s why it’s called smartphone. Point your camera toward the general direction and follow the arrows and voice leading you to your pets location. As you get closer you will also hear a beeping sound which will go faster and faster as you get near your pet. Once the beeping stops look around – your pet is next to you.

Unique Pet Paw Tracking System

If you think that we only offer pet facial recognition you'd be mistaken. When your pet goes missing we give you the chance to fill info about your pet’s paws and augment the Pet Facial Recognition feature with a totally new feature which will certainly lead to your pet. As you already know the pet can be best identified by the colors of its paws. So, all you have to do is fill in info about the colour of your pet’s paws or if you have a paw print you can even upload it and run it through our database and GEOTrax will find your pet immediately, thanks to its tiny, little paws.

Keep Your Pet in Your Neighborhood

The fact that the animals want freedom and want to jump over the fences and wander around isn’t new. But, this may be extremely dangerous and may lead to unwanted situations, such as having your pet taken to the pet shelters or stolen.

That’s why you should put boundaries – not physical of course. GEOTrax gives you the chance to set this virtual boundaries called SAFE zones and NO GO zones for your pets and get notified every time they cross the boundaries and get lost or stolen.
